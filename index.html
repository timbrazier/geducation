Guerrilla Education is an inspiringly optimistic educational consultancy company, whose mission is to education from the ground up. Bringing together innovative school based practice with a passion for developing 21st century learners, Guerrilla Education offers a refreshing approach to creativity, motivation and engagement.

Driven by the belief that committed individuals can and do make a difference, Guerrilla Education represents a lighthearted call to arms with a serious message about bravery, risk taking and the need for change.
