Working with individuals

From one to one coaching and mentoring, to intensive support, Jonathan can offer a tailored programme to suit the individual needs of teachers working in any phase of primary education. Jonathan has worked extensively with NQT’s, experienced teachers and senior leaders to develop and enhance their practice.

"An exceptionally inspiring talk. I have remembered what I love about teaching!! Creativity, fun, learning, motivation - Thank you very much."

Robyn Wright, Reigate Parish Church School

Some of the areas that Jonathan has worked on most recently include:

  • Planning
  • Effective questioning
  • Creative teaching through context
  • Child-led learning
  • Behaviour management
  • Engagement and motivation
  • Middle and senior leadership development

"After modelling a lesson, Jonathan worked with me on the planning process - he made it so simple and straightforward - I felt much more confident and left the session full of ideas. Thank you - it was brilliant!"

Y6 Teacher, St Joseph’s Catholic Primary, Rotherham
