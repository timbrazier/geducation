Guerrilla Teaching is a revolution. Not a flag-waving, drum-beating revolution, but an underground revolution. It’s not about changing policy or influencing government; it’s about doing what you know to be right, regardless of what you’re told. It’s a book its author never meant to write, but it was just too important not to.
