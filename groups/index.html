Working with groups

Whether it’s a staff meeting for 20, or a conference for 200, Jonathan is able to provide thought provoking and inspiring training. To date, his audiences have included teachers, teaching assistants, middle and senior leaders, Headteachers, Deputies, Assistant Heads, Governors, university students, parents, and children.

"Jonathan’s passion for teaching and for helping others is infectious. He is a skilled and dynamic presenter whose sessions are creative and inspired. I learned so much!"

Dr. Jonathan Doherty, Leeds Trinity University

Themes from some of his most recent events include:

  • Creativity
  • Creative teaching and learning
  • Child-led learning
  • Curiosity
  • Curriculum design
  • Initial Teacher Training
  • NQT development
  • Behaviour management
  • Engagement and motivation

"An outstanding presentation, enthusiastically delivered and crammed full of excellent ideas"

St Bernard’s Primary, Bolton
